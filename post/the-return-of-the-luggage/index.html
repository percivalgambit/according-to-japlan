The Return of the Luggage

13 July 2017

We started out the day by grabbing a quick breakfast from 7-Eleven, then we headed to Akihavara to look at all of the stores and arcades there. We started out by going to the Kanda Shrine, which is a shrine that caters to technophiles and geeks. We prayed at the shrine and got fortunes there. Afterwards, we looked at some of the stores in Akihabara selling anime merchandise and went to an arcade to play some of the crane games and Jubeat. We even won a plushie from one of the crane games!

At this point, it was noon, so we went back to the hostel to check if our luggage had arrived there. Our suitcases were there, so we brought them back up to our room and unpacked some of our things. After rejoicing about the return of our suitcases, we went back to Akihabara for lunch, which we got at the AKB48 Cafe. After lunch, we looked at more stores in Akihabara, including Don Quijote, a giant chain store. Then we want back to an arcade to play more Jubeat and Dance Dance Revolution. Finally, after we got tired of the arcade, we went back to the hostel to do laundry and sleep.
